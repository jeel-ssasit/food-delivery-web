About FoodieExpress

Connecting food lovers with the best restaurants in your city

Our Story

Founded in 2026, FoodieExpress started as a small idea to revolutionize the way people experience food delivery. What began as a passion project among friends has grown into India's leading food delivery platform, serving millions of customers across the country.

Our journey began when we realized that great food shouldn't be limited by time or distance. We set out to create a platform that connects hungry customers with the best local restaurants, ensuring every meal is delivered hot, fresh, and on time.

Today, we're proud to partner with over 50,000 restaurants and have delivered more than 100 million meals. But our story is just beginning - we're constantly innovating to make food delivery even better for everyone involved.

Join us as we continue to write the next chapter in our story, one delicious meal at a time.

Our Mission

To make delicious food accessible to everyone, everywhere, while supporting local restaurants and creating opportunities for food service professionals across India.

We believe that great food brings people together, and we're committed to:

  • Delivering exceptional food experiences
  • Supporting local restaurants and businesses
  • Ensuring fair wages for delivery partners
  • Promoting sustainable food practices
  • Innovating for better customer service
